HOLI FESTIVAL

3/1/18 - 3/2/18

"Festival of Colors"

INDIA

This celebration can be dated back to the fourth century.

Holi is a Hindu festival that signals the arrival of spring. The festival lasts for two days and is a celebration of fertility, color, love, and ultimately triumph of good over evil.

Holi is celebrated the most in India and Nepal and is recognized by the colored powders that are thrown during the festivities.
