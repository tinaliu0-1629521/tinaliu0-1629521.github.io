Cityscapes

Turkey

Istanbul


  • Istanbul, Turkey is a beautiful city bordered by the Sea of Marmara and the Black Sea.


  • Attractions include the Sultan Ahmed Mosque, Topkapi Palace and the Miniaturk.




Morocco

Chefchaouen


  • Chefchaouen, Morocco is nicknamed the Blue Pearl for the blue hue of all its buildings.


  • The town is lined with cafes and fantastic food at every corner.




Japan

Kyoto


  • Kyoto, Japan is located in the central part of the island.


  • The shrines and temples are certainly one of the most popular sights in the area along with their famous Bamboo Forest.
