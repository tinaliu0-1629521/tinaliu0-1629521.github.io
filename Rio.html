Rio Carnival

2/9/18 - 2/14/18

Rio de Janeiro

BRAZIL

The first festivals of Rio date back to 1723 and is considered the biggest carnival in the world.

This celebration is held every year before Lent to honor the gods and respect the great waters.

The Rio carnival parade is made up of many performers including flag bearers, floats, and drum queens who are all in colorful costumes.
