Northern Scandinavia

Salar de Uyuni

Bolivia


  • To view the rainy season, visit between December to April. To view the dry season, visit between May and November.


  • TThe salt flats are an amazing photo opportunity where visitors often play with perspective and depth of field.

Cappadocia


  • If you visit Cappadocia, viewing the hot air balloons is a must. The view from above is absolutely gorgeous.


  • Hiking and biking in the mountain ranges in the area is another way to experience the beautiful views in Cappadocia
